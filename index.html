Wheedle

(Synonoms: Scrounge, Finagle)

verb

Definition: To use soft words or flattery," usually for the purpose of persuading someone to do something or to give you something. It is often used disapprovingly, and is frequently followed by the word into, as in "wheedle one's way into favor."
